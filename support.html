Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I get an API key?

A: Visit console.anthropic.com, create a free account, and generate an API key. New users get $5 in free credits to start!

Q: Can I use the app without an API key?

A: Yes! You can track your reading progress, manage your library, and use all organizational features without AI Mode. AI features (summaries and characters) require an API key.

Q: How much do AI features cost?

A: AI features use pay-as-you-go pricing through Anthropic. A typical summary costs less than $0.01. The app itself is completely free!

Q: Is my reading data private?

A: Absolutely! All your books and progress are stored locally on your device. We don't collect or store any of your reading data on our servers.

Q: Can I change the summary language?

A: Yes! In Book Details ‚Üí Settings, you can choose English, Hindi, or Marathi for your summaries.

Q: What if the summary isn't accurate?

A: Tap "Regenerate Summary" to get a fresh version. The AI analyzes the book each time, so results may vary slightly.

Q: Can I export or share summaries?

A: Yes! Tap the share button (üì§) in the summary view to export via Messages, Mail, Notes, or any other app.

Q: The app crashes when generating summaries. What should I do?

A: Try these steps:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify your API key is correct in Settings
  • Restart the app
  • If the issue persists, contact support below

Q: Can I sync across devices?

A: Currently, data is stored locally on each device. Cloud sync is planned for a future update!

Troubleshooting

üîß Common Issues

  • Check internet connection
  • Try clearing cover cache in Settings
  • Restart the app

Text-to-speech not working:

  • Check device volume
  • Ensure the app has microphone permissions (Settings ‚Üí Privacy)
  • Restart the app
